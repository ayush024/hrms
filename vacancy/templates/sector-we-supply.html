Sector We Supply

Marigold Overseas Pvt. Ltd. has been providing manpower solutions to its clients in Poland, Cyprus, Malta, Romania, Saudi Arabia, U.A.E., Qatar, Malaysia, Bahrain, Kuwait, Oman and Middle East countries through the inception of the company. Our mission is to provide our clients and candidates with the excellent recruitment services based on mutual trust and the highest professional standards driven by quality and cost consciousness.
It is the most prominent factor for the employee to know the working condition, political condition, climatic condition, socio-cultural values, rules and regulation of their destination country for their adaptability towards work. So the orientation program is held to give them necessary information based on the recruitment. The orientation directs them to maintain good circumstance and motivates them to their duties and responsibilities.
